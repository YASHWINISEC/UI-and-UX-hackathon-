# Attendance Management System

## Overview
The Attendance Management System is a web-based platform designed to simplify and digitalize attendance tracking in educational institutions. It replaces manual record-keeping with an accurate, user-friendly, and efficient system. Along with attendance, the platform includes modules for dashboards, timetables, assignments, and announcements, making it a complete academic management solution.

## Features

### 1. Dashboard
The Dashboard serves as the central hub for users.
- Displays overall attendance percentage
- Shows total attended vs. total classes
- Displays upcoming classes
- Shows pending assignments and recent announcements
- Teachers can access attendance tools and assignment management options

### 2. Attendance Management
A dedicated section for recording and viewing attendance.

#### For Students:
- View daily, weekly, and monthly attendance
- Check subject-wise attendance
- Track attendance percentage
- Identify subjects with low attendance

#### For Teachers:
- Mark attendance quickly
- Edit or update previous entries
- Generate attendance reports
- View class-wise and subject-wise attendance

### 3. Timetable
Provides the complete academic schedule for users.
- Daily, weekly, or monthly view
- Shows subjects, faculty, timings, and room numbers
- Highlights current and upcoming classes
- Improves planning and time management

### 4. Assignment Management
A structured space for academic tasks.

#### Students:
- View assignments uploaded by teachers
- Download files or instructions
- Check deadlines and submission status
- Upload completed assignments

#### Teachers:
- Upload new assignments
- Set due dates and instructions
- Review submissions
- Provide feedback and grades

### 5. Announcements Page
Acts as the institution’s digital notice board.
- Displays important updates such as exams, events, and holidays
- Provides real-time announcements
- Ensures students do not miss critical information
